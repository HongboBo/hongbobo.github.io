Short Bio

I am a Senior Research Associate working on AI TAPs at CenSoF, University of Bristol. Before joining Bristol, I was a Research Associate working on the Data Science and Artificial Intelligence Programme at NIHR IO, Newcastle University. I have a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Bristol supervised by Professor Weiru Liu (Co-supervised with Dr. Ryan McConville and Prof. Jun Hong). I’m currently particularly interested in Data Science, Machine Learning and AI.
