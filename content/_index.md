## Short Bio

I am a Senior Research Associate working on AI TAPs at [CenSoF](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/fssl/research/sociodigital-futures/), University of Bristol. Before joining Bristol, I was a Research Associate working on the Data Science and Artificial Intelligence Programme at [NIHR IO](https://www.io.nihr.ac.uk/), Newcastle University. I have a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Bristol supervised by Professor [Weiru Liu](https://research-information.bris.ac.uk/en/persons/weiru-liu) (Co-supervised with Dr. [Ryan McConville](https://ryanmcconville.com/) and Prof. [Jun Hong](https://people.uwe.ac.uk/Person/JunHong)). I'm currently particularly interested in Data Science, Machine Learning and AI.
